This is Discuss.

A platform built for the discussion of Competitive programming, especially keeping the course structure of UG2K19 in mind. This platform is created solely for the purpose of sharing knowledge and learning through each other, so all articles are maintained by people of our batch.

This would serve as a place to put up editorials of OJ Assignments, discuss ideas, share tutorials, problem sets while simultaneously archiving them for further use.

There is no restriction to any user as far as posting on articles is concerned, but I believe that we can maintain the decorum of the platform and leave the spamming to other places. Genuine feedbacks are always encouraged.

Happy coding! :)
